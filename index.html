What is a Toaster Oven?

A toaster oven is a small electric oven that is used for toasting bread and other foods. Unlike regular toasters, a toaster oven can also bake, broil, and even roast, making it a versatile kitchen appliance.

Why Choose a Toaster Oven?

  • Compact size - perfect for small kitchens.
  • Energy-efficient compared to full-size ovens.
  • Multi-functional - bake, broil, and toast with one appliance.
